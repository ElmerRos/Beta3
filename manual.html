Lottery App Manual

Lottery App Manual (English)

Date Selection: The date input lets you pick one or multiple betting dates. Today is automatically assigned.

Tracks: Expand the “USA” or “Santo Domingo” sections. Mark the tracks you want. Some tracks will disable after cutoff time. If you pick “Venezuela,” combine it with a USA track to do Venezuelan plays. For “NY Horses,” you can enter any digits (1–4) and it will appear as “NY Horses.”

Plays Table: Each row has a Bet Number, which determines the game mode (Pulito, Pick 3, Win 4, Single Action, NY Horses, etc.). Straight/Box/Combo amounts can be added. The total for that row is displayed. The overall total is multiplied by your number of selected dates and tracks.

Single Action: Occurs if you pick a standard USA track (not Venezuela, not NY Horses) and input exactly 1 digit in Bet Number. Then the mode is “Single Action.”

NY Horses: If “New York Horses” is marked, any number of digits (1–4) results in “NY Horses.”

Buttons:

  • Add Play: Adds a new row in the table.
  • Wizard: Opens the Quick Entry Window for bulk or random plays.
  • Remove Last Play: Removes the final row from the table.
  • Reset Form: Clears everything (dates, tracks, table).
  • Generate Ticket: Shows the Ticket Preview window, where you can confirm, print, or share the ticket.

Quick Entry Window (Wizard): You can add multiple plays quickly, lock amounts (Straight, Box, Combo), generate Quick Picks, do Round Down sequences, or permute digits. Then click “Add Main” to pass them to the main table or “Generate” to create a ticket directly.

Ticket Preview: Upon generating a ticket, you see a preview with all plays, total amount, and a QR code. You can edit (return to the form) or confirm and print/share. A unique Ticket Number is assigned once you confirm.

Manual Completo de la App de Loterías (Español)

Selección de Fechas: El campo de fecha permite escoger una o varias fechas de apuesta. Por defecto, hoy se asigna automáticamente.

Tracks: Expande las secciones “USA” o “Santo Domingo” y marca las loterías que desees. Algunos tracks se desactivan tras su cutoff. Si eliges “Venezuela,” combina con un track de USA. Si eliges “New York Horses,” puedes ingresar 1-4 dígitos y aparecerá como “NY Horses.”

Tabla de Jugadas: Cada fila tiene un Bet Number que determina la modalidad (Pulito, Pick 3, Win 4, Single Action, NY Horses, etc.). Puedes añadir montos en Straight, Box, Combo. El total de cada fila se muestra. El total global se multiplica por las fechas y tracks marcados.

Single Action: Sucede si eliges un track USA (que no sea Venezuela ni NY Horses) y pones 1 dígito exacto en Bet Number.

NY Horses: Si está marcado “New York Horses,” cualquier Bet Number de 1–4 dígitos resultará en la modalidad “NY Horses.”

Botones:

  • Add Play: Agrega una nueva fila en la tabla.
  • Wizard: Abre la ventana de “Quick Entry” para varias jugadas rápidas o aleatorias.
  • Remove Last Play: Quita la última fila.
  • Reset Form: Limpia todo (fechas, tracks, tabla).
  • Generate Ticket: Muestra la ventana de previsualización del ticket, donde puedes confirmar, imprimir o compartir.

Ventana “Wizard” (Quick Entry): Permite añadir jugadas en bloque, bloquear montos (Straight, Box, Combo), hacer Quick Picks, Round Down, o permutar dígitos. Al final, “Add Main” envía las jugadas a la tabla principal, o “Generate” crea el ticket.

Vista Previa del Ticket: Al generar el ticket, ves la lista de jugadas, el total y un código QR. Puedes editar (volver al formulario) o confirmar y así generar un número de ticket único y compartir/imprimir.

Manyèl Aplikasyon Lòtri (Kreyòl)

Chwazi Dat: Zòn dat la pèmèt ou pran youn oswa plizyè jou parye. Jodi a mete otomatikman.

Tracks: Gade seksyon “USA” oswa “Santo Domingo.” Gen kous ki ap dezaktive apre cutoff. Si ou chwazi “Venezuela,” dwe ak USA. “New York Horses” pèmèt ou antre 1–4 chif, li pa “Single Action.”

Tab Pari: Chak liy gen Bet Number, li detèmine mòd jwe (Pulito, Pick 3, Win 4, Single Action, Horses...). Ou ka mete Straight, Box, Combo. Total jeneral miltipliye ak dat + track.

Single Action: Si track USA (pa Venezuela ni Horses) ak exactly 1 chif => “Single Action.”

NY Horses: Si “New York Horses” make, 1–4 chif = “NY Horses.”

Bouton:

  • Add Play: Ajoute yon liy.
  • Wizard: Fenèt Quick Entry pou mete plizyè parye vit.
  • Remove Last Play: Retire dènye liy la.
  • Reset Form: Efase tout bagay (dat, track, tablo).
  • Generate Ticket: Montre fenèt Preview ak opsyon konfim/print/share.

Fenèt “Wizard” (Quick Entry): Ou ka antre parye an blok, kenbe (Straight/Box/Combo), Quick Pick, Round Down, Permute. “Add Main” voye parye yo nan tablo prensipal, “Generate” kreye tikè a.

Preview Ticket: Montre lis parye yo, total ak QR. Ou ka Modifye (retounen) oswa Konfime pou bay yon nimewo tikè inik epi pataje/imprime.
